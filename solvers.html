PCG with DIC Preconditioning for Ax = b

Given: $$ A= \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 2 & 3\ 2 & 5 & 1\ 3 & 1 & 8 \end{bmatrix} $$

A = [[4, 2, 3], [2, 5, 1], [3, 1, 8]] b = [10, 13, 24] x0 = [3, 3, 3]

1. DIC Preconditioning:

  • L = [[4, 0, 0], [2, 5, 0], [3, 1, 8]]
  • D = [[4, 0, 0], [0, 5, 0], [0, 0, 8]]
  • Solve D * y = L * b: y = [2.5, 2.6, 3]

2. Initialization:

  • r0 = b - Ax0 = [1, 7, 9]
  • p0 = r0

3. Iteration Loop:

Iteration α_i x_i q_i β_i p_i r_i
1 - - - - - -
2 - - - - - -
3 - - - - - -
... ... ... ... ... ... ...
$$
\begin{align*}
\alpha_i &= \frac{\mathbf{r}_i^T \mathbf{r}_i}{\mathbf{p}i^T \mathbf{A} \mathbf{p}i} \
\mathbf{x}{i+1} &= \mathbf{x}i + \alpha_i \mathbf{p}i \
\mathbf{q}i &= \mathbf{D}^{-1} \mathbf{L} (\mathbf{b} - \mathbf{A} \mathbf{x}{i+1}) \
\beta_i &= \frac{\mathbf{q}{i+1}^T \mathbf{q}{i+1}}{\mathbf{r}i^T \mathbf{r}i} \
\mathbf{p}{i+1} &= \mathbf{q}{i+1} + \beta_i \mathbf{p}i \
\mathbf{r}{i+1} &= \mathbf{b} - \mathbf{A} \mathbf{x}{i+1}.
\end{align*}
$$

Solution:

Fill in the table with calculated values for α_i, x_i, q_i, β_i, p_i, and r_i at each iteration until convergence (||r_i|| < threshold). The final x_i is the solution vector.
