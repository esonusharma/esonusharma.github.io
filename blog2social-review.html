Blog2Social Review

Posted on: Wed Aug 12 2020 05:30:00 GMT+0530 (India Standard Time)

Blog2Social Social Media Auto Post Scheduler is a unique and interesting social media plugin with a lot of options.

So what is unique about it? “You can do a lot of social media marketing tasks with a single plugin, without bloat on your blog.”

Adenion is the company behind it.

I started my blogging journey in 2016 with WordPress and found Blog2Social in its early days.

Blog2Social was a glorius plugin then and now even better.

What is Blog2social?

  • Blog2social is a WordPress plugin and web app.
  • Free WordPress Social Auto Poster Plugin.
  • Can auto-share WordPress posts on social media.

What can you accomplish with Blog2Social?

  • cross-sharing on social media websites.
  • automatic sharing of blog posts on your connected and chosen social media accounts as soon as they are published on your blog.
  • you can edit your posts before re-publishing, add hashtags for increasing the visibility to the targeted audience, add handles to share your masterpiece with the experts.
  • setup best times for sharing your blog posts on social networks, it is necessary to share at the optimum timings because you need an active social media user to pay attention to your post otherwise the post will be lost in thousands of messages and posts the users get in their social accounts. With Blog2Social, you get pre-defined times to choose from.
  • You can choose to schedule your post for sharing on multiple profiles, pages, and groups once, or repeatedly.
  • Choose an image for your posts.
  • It is important to keep records of your scheduling and sharing activities. Blog2Social can do it out of the box.

Inherently, Blog2Social is a social media automation tool, helping you in social media marketing of your content without bothering you, as it serves as a set and forget plugin.

  • Blog2Social has both free and premium options. Details can be found here
  • You can also try the premium version of Blog2Social free for 30-days here
  • You can also try their webapp
  • Easily share content directly from your browser using Blog2Social Browser Extension
  • Know more about Blog2Social

Last but not the least, don’t use Blog2Social nulled or pirated version, that’s not cool, also it helps strangers to inject malicious code on your website and destroy your hard work. Instead, you can use the free version of the Blog2Social social media automation plugin or trial version to test it out before buying.

You can expect quality support from the Blog2Social Support team as the parent firm Adenion is a well-established name.
