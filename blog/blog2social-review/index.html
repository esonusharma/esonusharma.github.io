What is Blog2social?

  • Blog2social is a WordPress plugin and web app.
  • Free WordPress Social Auto Poster Plugin.
  • Can auto-share WordPress posts on social media.

What can you accomplish with Blog2Social?

  • cross-sharing on social media websites.
  • automatic sharing of blog posts on your connected and chosen social media accounts as soon as they are published on your blog.
  • you can edit your posts before re-publishing, add hashtags for increasing the visibility to the targeted audience, add handles to share your masterpiece with the experts.
  • setup best times for sharing your blog posts on social networks, it is necessary to share at the optimum timings because you need an active social media user to pay attention to your post otherwise the post will be lost in thousands of messages and posts the users get in their social accounts. With Blog2Social, you get pre-defined times to choose from.
  • You can choose to schedule your post for sharing on multiple profiles, pages, and groups once, or repeatedly.
  • Choose an image for your posts.
  • It is important to keep records of your scheduling and sharing activities. Blog2Social can do it out of the box.
