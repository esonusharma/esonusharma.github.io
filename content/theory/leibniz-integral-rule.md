Leibniz integral rule gives a formula for differentiation of a definite integral whose limits are functions of the differential variable.

$$
\frac{d}{d t} \int_{V(t)} f(\vec{x}, t) d V=\int_{V(t)} \frac{\partial f}{\partial t} d V+\int_{A(t)} f \vec{u}_{A} \cdot \hat{n} dA
$$
