### Total pressure

Total pressure is the pressure a fluid would have if brought to rest isentropically.

Total pressure (stagnation pressure) = static pressure + dynamic pressure + hydrostatic pressure

$$
p_{stag}=p_{static}+\frac{1}{2}(\rho u^{2})+\rho gh~(along~a~streamline)
$$
